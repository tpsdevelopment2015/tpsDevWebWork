Established 1990

Since 1990, Irondale Industrial Contractors has been specializing in industrial construction. We can offer total design/build turnkey solutions to our customers. As a merit shop contractor, IIC works both nationwide and internationally, while maintaining safety and quality control standards which are among the toughest in the industry.
