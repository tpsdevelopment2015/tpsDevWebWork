Established 1990

For 25 years IIC personnel have executed steel mill/foundry projects for every major producer in nearly every major plant. These projects encompass all aspects of the steel/metals production process. IIC has long and successful relationships with every steel making OEM. Steel making equipment setting is one of our specialties. We have completed new installations, equipment rebuilds, modifications, and quick turn-around outage projects. We have a vast reference list of projects that include; EAF’s, LMF’s, VTD’s, CCM’s, reheat furnaces, specialty furnaces, rolling mills, process lines, auxiliary equipment, and environmental equipment.
