Lime/Cement

IIC has installed more lime production kilns than any US based contractor. We’ve executed numerous pyro process lines for both the lime & cement production industries. Pre-heater towers, calciners, raw material mills/presses, blending silos, raw material transport equipment, kiln equipment, dutwork, coal mill, coolers, as well as environmental equipment comprise our vast project reference list. We’ve worked for all of the lime and cement process OEM’s. We’ve successfully completed lime & cement projects throughout the USA. Capital projects for new facility installations as well as during shutdowns have been safely and successfully completed by IIC personnel.
